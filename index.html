1893 Espresso - Medium Roast Coffee

Crafted for modern espresso lovers, 1893 Espresso is a smooth, full-bodied medium roast coffee with a naturally sweet profile that sets it apart from traditional espresso blends. Designed to shine with milk, its naturally caramelized sugars pair beautifully in lattes, cappuccinos, and other espresso-based drinks, while remaining balanced and flavorful on its own.

Kenyan Single Origin - Medium Roast Coffee

Our Kenyan coffee is celebrated for its vibrant, complex flavor — full-bodied, bright, and naturally sweet. Grown from unique genetic varieties developed specifically for Kenya’s rich soil and high-altitude micro-climate, these single-origin coffee beans showcase everything that makes East African coffee truly exceptional.

Safari Blend - Medium Roast Coffee

Our best-selling Safari blend is a bright, naturally sweet medium roast coffee crafted from high-quality, antioxidant-rich African coffee beans. Smooth and balanced, it shines on its own or with milk, offering subtle milk chocolate and caramel coffee notes.

Serengeti Blend - Dark Roast Coffee

Our Serengeti Blend is ideal for dark roast lovers who enjoy a rich, full-bodied cup without bitterness. Named after the iconic Serengeti grasslands of East Africa, this bold African coffee delivers molasses and chocolate coffee notes with a slight tangy sweetness, making it a dream pairing for milk or cream.

The history of Kenyan Coffee in the world

Imagine waking up to the bright burst of blackcurrant and juicy citrus dancing on your tongue—that’s Kenyan coffee at its finest. High above the clouds in Nyeri, Kirinyaga, and Kiambu, volcanic soil and cool misty air create beans bursting with life, clarity, and irresistible sweetness. Pure magic in every sip.

From the hands of resilient women farmers on Kenya’s emerald slopes comes coffee that doesn’t just wake you up—it lifts you. Clean, mold-free, antioxidant-rich AA beans deliver smooth, focused energy and a gentle warmth that lingers. One cup, and you taste the pride, the purpose, the heartbeat of Kenya.
